Library

Welcome

📚 Check out books

Borrow books and other materials for two weeks.

You must get a library card to check out materials.

🔎 Find books

Browse and search for books and other materials.

Review book recommendations and reading lists on the library website.

📮 Return books

Place books and other materials in the slot of the nearby wooden box. Library helpers will check in the items and return them to the shelves.

🔄 Renew books

Books and other materials may be renewed by a library helper after services on Sundays. Alternatively, you may contact the librarian.

💳 Get a library card

Fill out either the online Library Card Signup Form or the paper form.

New cards should be available within a week. Your card will be stored in the 🗃 filing box.

❓ Help

  • Ask a library helper on Sundays (look for a person wearing a badge)
  • Write a note on the provided paper
  • Contact the librarian

📧 Contact

For futher help or questions, email the librarian at ecclibrarybloomington@gmail.com.
