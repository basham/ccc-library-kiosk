Library

Welcome

📚 Check out books

Borrow or renew items for three weeks.

You must get a library card to check out items.

🔎 Find books

Browse and search for items.

Review book recommendations and reading lists on the library website.

📮 Return books

Place items in the slot of the nearby wooden box. Library helpers will check in the items and return them to the shelves.

Please contact the librarian if an item has been lost.

💳 Get a library card

Fill out either the online Library Card Signup Form or the paper form.

New cards should be available within a week. Your card will be stored in the 🗃 filing box.

❓ Help

  • Ask a library helper on Sundays (look for a person wearing a badge)
  • Write a note on the provided paper
  • Contact the librarian

📧 Contact

For further help or questions, email the librarian at ecclibrarybloomington@gmail.com.
